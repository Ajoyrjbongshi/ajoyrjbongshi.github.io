About Me

I am Ajoy Rjbongshi, a mathematical sciences graduate student at Clemson University. I completed my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Statistics from the University of Dhaka. My research interests include biostatistics, machine learning, Bayesian statistics, public health, survival analysis, causal inference, and mental health. I currently serve as a Graduate Teacher of Record at Clemson University, where I independently teach STAT 2220, and I previously worked as a graduate teaching assistant supporting various statistics courses. Outside of academia, I enjoy traveling to new places and playing video games in my free time.
